\noindent If $a \in A$ is the infimum of $A$ then it is unique.\\

\noindent Proof:\\
Suppose we have tow infimums of $(a, a') \in A^2$\\
$\Rightarrow \forall x \in A, a \preceq x \ and \
a' \preceq x$\\
$\Rightarrow a \preceq a' \ and \ a' \ \preceq a$\\
$\Rightarrow a = a'$
