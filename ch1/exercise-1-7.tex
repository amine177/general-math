\noindent Let $ P $ be the set of even numbers. Proove that\\
\[\phi: \begin{cases}
  \mathbb{N} \rightarrow P\\
  n \mapsto 2n
\end{cases}\]\\
Is bijective.\\\\
\noindent
$\forall y \in P, \exists! x = \frac{y}{2} $ | $ 2x = y $\\
$\Rightarrow \phi $ is bijective\\
$\Rightarrow |P| = |\mathbb{N}|$\\
